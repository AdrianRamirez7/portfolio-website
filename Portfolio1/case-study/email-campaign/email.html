Kiwanis Club of Sweetwater: Community Website Redesign & Optimization

A fully responsive WordPress website built to increase community engagement, donations, and local visibility for a 50+ year service organization in National City, CA.

Project Overview

Developed and managed the official website for the Kiwanis Club of Sweetwater (National City, CA), a long-standing community service organization focused on youth programs and local outreach initiatives.

As the Content Editor and site lead, I designed and built the website using WordPress and Elementor, creating a modern, fully responsive digital presence aligned with the organization’s mission and brand identity. In addition to page builder workflows, I implemented custom HTML and CSS sections to enhance layout flexibility, improve visual hierarchy, and devliver more tailored design components beyond standard theme capabilities.

The site includes structured donation workflows, dynamic blog content, SEO optimization, and performance enhancements to ensure accessibility across all devices and browsers. Special attention was given to content clarity, accessibility, and conversion-focused layout to support fundraising and volunteer engagement.

Search visibility was significantly improved through Yoast SEO optimization, achieving an estimated 90–95% SEO performance score across primary pages and improving local discoverability in National City searches.

Performance optimization using Airlift delivered a 100% Google PageSpeed performance score, ensuring fast load times and improved user retention.

Key Highlights

  • 100% Google Pagespeed Performance Score

    Optimized via Airlift plugin for maximum speed, caching efficiency, and performance stability

  • Fully Responsive Design

    Modular template system enabled rapid campaign deployment with reusable components

  • 90–95% SEO Optimization Score (Yoast SEO)

    Implemented structured metadata, schema, keyword targeting, and on-page SEO best practices to improve local search visibility

  • Integrated Donation System

    Built secure, user-friendly donation pages to support fundraising campaigns and community initiatives

  • Content Strategy & Blog Management

    Developed and maintained blog posts to highlight events, community impact stories, and organizational updates

  • Conversion-Focused Layouts

    Structured CTAs, event promotion sections, and donation prompts to increase engagement and action

  • CMS Administration & Ongoing Content Updates

    Managed site content, media optimization, plugin updates, and structural improvements as acting Web Administrator

  • Accessibility & Cross-Browser Compatibility

    Ensured consistent rendering and usability across major browsers and mobile platforms
